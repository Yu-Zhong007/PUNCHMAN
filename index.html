STEP 1

Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to Phantom.com.

STEP 2

Get Some sol

have sol in your wallet to switch to $tyson. If you don’t have any sol, you can buy directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

STEP 3

Go to raydium

Connect to raydium. Go to raydium.io in google chrome or on the browser inside your Phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $tyson token address into raydium, select TysonOnSoL, & confirm.

STEP 4

Switch sol for $tyson
